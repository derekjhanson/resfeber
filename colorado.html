Colorado

October 21, 2015

For our Honeymoonaversary we chose the Denver region to get a tast of fall while our fellow residents of Austin continued to sweat it out in 90 degree temperatures.

Fall Foliage

We found some nice fall colors on the Cherry Creek trail where we strolled on Saturday. The trail runs from Downtown Denver for many miles. Many of the oaks and maples were just past their peak colors and put on a nice show for us. Walking around Fort Collins also provided some nice views, especially on the river.

And who doesn't want some quaking Aspens?

 

Fort Collins

They give out free beer in Fort Collins. Enough said.
